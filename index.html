About Me

My first experiences with technology included gaming on the boxy PC and when I learned that I could get play a part with the things that gave me so much joy, you can bet that I customised my Neopets profile with a fairy wand cursor. Curiousity played a large part in my interest in tech and changing that single cursor design lead me down a path of developing interfaces for friends to use, programming a robotic arm, and helping spark that same curiousity in others through teaching.

I want to move forward by persisting in developing my abilities, diving into the knowledge of others, and providing my intentions of accessibility through my work. People have always been at the center of my work and I would love the opportunity to be a part of a team that helps create a positive impact on how information is communicated to everyone. Whether this is through user experience design, IT infrastructure, or making code accessible - I want the chance to be a part of this.
